How I work

I’m comfortable leading when the path isn’t obvious—framing ambiguity into priorities, tradeoffs, and a plan the org can execute. I run cross-functional programs across marketing, sales, operations, legal, and customer service, aligning incentives so delivery isn’t a handoff marathon. I ship changes, not slides. My bias is toward launches, learning in production, and systems teams trust when the edge cases show up.
